Chicago summers are short. Make every patio hour count.

SummertimeChi tells you whether a bar's outdoor patio is in the sun or shade — right now, and at any time of day. No more arriving at your favorite spot only to find you're sitting in a shadow all afternoon.

HOW IT WORKS
─────────────
Using real-time solar position calculations and 3D LIDAR building data from Chicago's own mapping systems, SummertimeChi draws accurate shadow polygons directly on a 3D map. You can see exactly which patios the sun is hitting at this moment.

THE TIME SLIDER
───────────────
Planning a meetup at 4pm? Drag the time slider to see what the sun situation will look like before you leave the house. Simulate any hour from 6 AM to 11 PM without changing your phone's clock.

FIND YOUR KIND OF SUN
──────────────────────
Map Tab — Browse an interactive 3D map of Chicago with color-coded pins showing each patio's current sun status. Tap any pin for a full day-by-day timeline.

Search Tab — Filter bars by "In Sun Now," "In Shade," name, or neighborhood. Covers hundreds of confirmed outdoor patio bars across every Chicago neighborhood: River North, Wicker Park, Wrigleyville, Logan Square, Old Town, Lincoln Park, Lakeview, the Loop, and more.

Favorites Tab — Save your go-to spots and get weather context (cloud cover + patio suitability) all in one place.

SUN ALERTS
──────────
Enable alerts on any favorite bar and get notified the moment its patio moves into — or out of — direct sunlight.

BEAUTIFUL 3D VISUALIZATION
────────────────────────────
Toggle between a flat overview and a tilted 3D perspective with Apple's realistic building models. Shadow overlays update in real time as you pan and zoom.

BUILT FOR CHICAGO
──────────────────
SummertimeChi focuses entirely on Chicago's patio scene — not a generic nationwide bar finder. Every bar on the map has a confirmed outdoor patio. Data is sourced from OpenStreetMap and ArcGIS Chicago 3D Buildings and refreshed regularly.

Whether you're chasing the afternoon sun at Sheffield's or hunting for shade on a scorching August Saturday, SummertimeChi helps you pick the right patio at the right time.
